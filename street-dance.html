Project Overview

This project presents a structured comparison between street dance and four traditional cultural dance forms: Tamil, Hindi, Malayalam, and Telugu. Each cultural page follows a consistent layout to highlight identity and tradition.

The pages include:

  • A cultural header featuring the flag and language script
  • An introduction to the traditional dance style
  • A comparison section between street dance and the cultural dance
  • Two embedded videos showing both dance styles
  • A comment section for user interaction

The aim was to create a visually consistent, culturally respectful, and easy-to-navigate website that helps users understand how different dance traditions compare to modern street dance.
